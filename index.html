Crystal Collector

Good Luck Friends!


Instructions

There are four crystals displayed at the bottom of the page. You are shown a random number at the start of the game. When you click on a crystal, it will add a specific amount of points to your total score. Your game will hide this amount until you click a crystal. When you click one, your counter will change. You win if your total score matches the random number from the beginning of the game. You lose if your score goes above the random number. The game restarts whenever you win or lose. Good Luck!
